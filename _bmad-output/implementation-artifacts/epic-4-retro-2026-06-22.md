## What went well

- **FR-8/9/10/11 largely met** — Dashboard, ranking, reports, export delivered as a cohesive operator loop.
- **Review caught export bugs** — Stale export while refetching, toast fallback, in-flight export — patched before Epic 5.
- **Intentional period semantics documented** — Rolling 7-day dashboard vs calendar UTC reports avoids silent metric mismatch.
- **Empty states** — Dashboard and reports both guide operator when no activities/registrations exist.
- **Click-through navigation** — MetricTiles deep-link into filtered clients/activities.

## What we learned

- **Aggregation cost is real** — Many sequential DB queries per dashboard/report request; acceptable at MVP scale, logged for batch/cache later.
- **Filter dropdown caps matter** — Activity/community options capped at 100 activities; Epic 6 improved catalog filters but pagination defer remains.
- **Follow-up coverage definition evolved** — Epic 4 used `LeadStatus != New`; Epic 5 extended with WhatsApp/campaign timeline events in reports.
- **Campaign block stub was correct** — Report UI placeholder until Epic 5 enabled campaign results data.
